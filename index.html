About SNHU's online IT program

Posted 1/4/2019

SNHU is a college based in New Hampshire. It offers an online BS in IT. To be accepted, it is enough to have a highschool diploma, you need not have a regents diploma. It's full time program is three and a half years. There are 6 terms in a year ,each one being 8 weeks. There are about 30 hours of work in a week. 120 credits earns you the degree. You can enter with upto 90 credits and then get the degree in a year, but the credits must have been earned from an accredited school and for a subject relating to IT. They also must be sent directly from the school to the college. Alternatively the college wil accept them as long as they are still sealed in the original envelope they were sent in.
